1. Service Description

BEEZEE is a workforce management platform that helps businesses organize daily operations. The Service provides:

  • Task management — create, assign, and track tasks for employees
  • Shift tracking — record employee work sessions with start and end times
  • Break management — track breaks during active shifts
  • Shift scheduling — plan and publish employee schedules in advance
  • Notifications — alert employees about task assignments, schedule changes, and operational events
  • Task templates — reusable templates with optional recurring schedules (daily or weekly)
  • Photo documentation — upload and manage task-related photos as proof of completion
  • Inventory requests — employees can request needed inventory items with priority and photos
  • Statistics — managers can view operational summaries and workforce analytics

The Service is available on iOS and Android via the BEEZEE mobile application.
