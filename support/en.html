Support

Get in touch

The fastest way to reach us is by email. We aim to reply within one business day.

Email biznest.platform@gmail.com

What to include

To help us help you faster, please include where relevant:

  • The email address you use to sign in to BEEZEE
  • Your role (Manager or Employee) and your business name
  • Your device (iPhone or Android) and the app version (Settings → About)
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected to happen
  • A screenshot if the issue is visual

Common topics

  • Setting up a business account. New business accounts are created on request — email us and we'll get you set up.
  • Adding employees. Employees don't self-register. The Manager invites them from inside the app.
  • Resetting your password. Use "Forgot password" on the sign-in screen to receive a one-time code by email.
  • Account deletion or data export. Email us with the request and we'll process it within the timelines set out in our Privacy Policy.

Privacy and legal
